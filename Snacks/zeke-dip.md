# Zeke Dip

## Ingredients
* 2 bunches green 
* 5 cloves garlic
* 2 jalapenos
* 2 cups spinach
* 16 oz sour cream
* Chicken Bouillon to taste


## Steps
* Blacken and de-seed jalapenos
* Roast the green onions and garlic
* Saute spinach
* Blend every thing and season to taste


## Notes
* Can sub half of the sour cream for greek yogurt
